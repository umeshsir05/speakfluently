❓ Interrogative Sentences (प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य)

Interrogative sentences are used to ask questions. They end with a question mark (?). There are two main types:

  • Yes/No Questions – जिनका उत्तर 'हाँ' या 'नहीं' में होता है (Helping verbs से शुरू)
  • Wh-Questions – जिनमें Wh-word (what, where, when, why, who, whom, whose, which, how) का प्रयोग होता है।

📌 Yes/No Questions (Helping Verbs Interrogative)

इन प्रश्नों की शुरुआत Helping Verb (is, am, are, was, were, do, does, did, has, have, had, will, shall, can, could, may, might, should, would, must) से होती है।

🔹 Structure

Formula: Helping Verb + Subject + Main Verb + Object ?
Example: Is she writing a letter?

🔹 Helping Verbs List for Yes/No Questions

Helping VerbUsed ForExample
is/am/arePresent Continuous, Passive, etc.Is he coming?
was/werePast Continuous, PassiveWere they playing?
do/doesPresent SimpleDo you like tea?
didPast SimpleDid she call you?
has/havePresent PerfectHave you eaten?
hadPast PerfectHad he left?
will/shallFutureWill they come?
can/couldAbility/PermissionCan you swim?
may/mightPossibility/PermissionMay I come in?
should/would/mustAdvice/Request/ObligationShould I go?

क्या तुमने ताजमहल देखा है?
Will you help me?
क्या तुम मेरी मदद करोगे?

📌 Wh-Questions (प्रश्नवाचक शब्दों वाले वाक्य)

इन प्रश्नों की शुरुआत Wh-word (What, Where, When, Why, Who, Whom, Whose, Which, How) से होती है। ये open-ended questions होते हैं।

🔹 Structure

Formula: Wh-word + Helping Verb + Subject + Main Verb + Object ?
Example: Why are you crying?

🔹 Wh-Words and Their Uses

Wh-WordUse (Hindi)Example
Whatक्या (thing, action)What is your name?
Whereकहाँ (place)Where do you live?
Whenकब (time)When will you come?
Whyक्यों (reason)Why are you sad?
Whoकौन (person - subject)Who broke the glass?
Whomकिसे / किसको (person - object)Whom did you meet?
Whoseकिसका (possession)Whose book is this?
Whichकौन सा (choice)Which color do you like?
Howकैसे (manner)How do you go to school?
How manyकितने (countable)How many students are present?
How muchकितना (uncountable)How much water do you drink?
How longकितनी देरHow long will you stay?
How farकितनी दूरHow far is the station?
How oftenकितनी बारHow often do you exercise?

📝 Special Case: Question without Helping Verb (Who as Subject)

जब प्रश्न Subject से शुरू होता है (जैसे Who, Which, What), तो Helping Verb का प्रयोग नहीं होता।
Structure: Who/Which/What + Main Verb + Object ?
Examples:
- Who broke the window? (किसने खिड़की तोड़ी?)
- Which train goes to Delhi? (कौन सी ट्रेन दिल्ली जाती है?)
- What happened yesterday? (कल क्या हुआ?)

🔹 Negative Interrogative Sentences

प्रश्नों को नकारात्मक बनाने के लिए 'not' का प्रयोग करते हैं।

Don't you like coffee?
क्या तुम्हें कॉफी पसंद नहीं है?
Why didn't you come?
तुम क्यों नहीं आए?
Hasn't she finished her work?
क्या उसने अपना काम पूरा नहीं किया है?

✍️ Practice Exercises

Change the following sentences into interrogative (Yes/No or Wh- as indicated):

1. She is reading a book. (Yes/No) → ____________________
2. They went to the park. (Yes/No) → ____________________
3. He has completed his homework. (Yes/No) → ____________________
4. You like pizza. (Yes/No) → ____________________
5. The train is arriving at 5 PM. (When) → ____________________
6. He lives in Mumbai. (Where) → ____________________
7. She is crying because she failed. (Why) → ____________________
8. This pen belongs to Rohan. (Whose) → ____________________
9. She met the President. (Whom) → ____________________
10. He can speak French. (Can) → ____________________
Answers:
1. Is she reading a book?
2. Did they go to the park?
3. Has he completed his homework?
4. Do you like pizza?
5. When is the train arriving?
6. Where does he live?
7. Why is she crying?
8. Whose pen is this?
9. Whom did she meet?
10. Can he speak French?
Remember: For Yes/No questions, answer is 'Yes' or 'No'. For Wh-questions, answer is a complete sentence. Always put question mark at the end.
