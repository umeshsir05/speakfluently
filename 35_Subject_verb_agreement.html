📖 Subject-Verb Agreement (Syntax)

Sentence Structure: Subject + Verb

Subject

Subject हमेशा Noun या Pronoun ही होता है।

Singular Subjects: He, She, It
Plural Subjects: We, You, They

Verb Forms

Singular Verb: is, was, has, Verb + s/es
Plural Verb: are, were, have
Note:
- Noun के अंत में s/es जोड़ने पर वह Plural बन जाता है।
- Verb के अंत में s/es जोड़ने पर वह Singular बन जाती है।
1. A dog chase / chases a cat.
2. Dogs chase / chases cats.

Rule: Noun + and + Noun

जब दो subjects "and" से जुड़े हों:

  • Plural verb → जब दोनों nouns अलग-अलग हों।
  • Singular verb → जब दोनों nouns एक single unit के रूप में हों।
Gold and silver are precious metals.
Water and oil do not mix.
Fire and ice is a famous poem.
Bread and butter is my main worry.
Dal, bati and churma is a popular dish in Rajasthan.
The horse and the carriage is waiting outside.
Early to bed and early to rise makes a man healthy, wealthy and wise.
To take pay and then not to work is dishonest.

Fixed Pairs (Always Singular)

Rise and fall
Slow and steady
Law and order
Rice and curry

Rules for Singular Verb

1. When two nouns refer to the same person
2. Article first noun के साथ आएगा, दूसरा noun same व्यक्ति को बताएगा। → Verb singular होगी।

The teacher and singer has invited me to his birthday party.
The poet and philosopher is coming here.
The poet and the philosopher are coming here. (Two different persons)
My friend and guide lives in the U.S.A. (Same person)
My friend and my guide live in the U.S.A. (Two persons)
Note: If the subject has each or every, singular verb is used.
Each boy and girl is willing to join this competition.
Every Peter, Tom, and Harry has a mobile nowadays.
Every leaf and flower proclaims the glory of God.
