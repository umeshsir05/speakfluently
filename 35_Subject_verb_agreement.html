Subject Verb Agreement



**Sentence Structure**

Subject + Verb


Subject:

- Singular
- Plural


-
Verb:

- Singular
- Plural



Subject

-Subject हमेशा Noun या Pronoun ही होता है।

Singular Subjects

- He
- She
- It

Plural Subjects

- We
- You
- They


Verb Forms

Singular Verb

- is
- was
- has
- Verb + s/es → Singular

Plural Verb

- are
- were
- have


Note-

- Noun के अंत में s/es जोड़ने पर वह **Plural** बन जाता है।
- Verb के अंत में s/es जोड़ने पर वह **Singular** बन जाती है।

Examples

1. A dog chase / chases a cat.
2. Dogs chase / chases cats.**


Rule: Noun + and + Noun

जब दो subjects "and" से जुड़े हों:

Plural verb
→ जब दोनों nouns अलग-अलग हों।

Singular verb
→ जब दोनों nouns एक single unit के रूप में हों।

Examples

1. Gold and silver is / are precious metals.
2. Water and oil do / does not mix.
3. Fire and ice is / are a famous poem.
4. Bread and butter is / are my main worry.
5. Dal, bati and churma is / are a popular dish in Rajasthan.


More Examples

1. The horse and the carriage is / are waiting outside.

2. Early to bed and early to rise ___ a man healthy, wealthy and wise. (a) make (b)makes (c) have made (d) NOTA

3. To take pay and then not to work is / are dishonest.


Fixed Pairs (Always Singular)

- Rise and fall
- Slow and steady
- Law and order
- Rice and curry

Singular verb


Rule: When two nouns refer to the same person

→ Article first noun के साथ आएगा, दूसरा noun same व्यक्ति को बताएगा। → Verb singular होगी।

Examples

1. The teacher and singer has / have invited me to his birthday party.

2. The poet and philosopher is / are coming here.**

3. The poet and the philosopher is / are coming here. (Here both nouns refer to two different persons.)

4. My friend and guide live / lives in the U.S.A. (Same person → singular verb)

5. My friend and my guide live / lives in the U.S.A. (Two persons → plural verb)


Note

If the subject has each or every, singular verb is used.

Examples

1. Each boy and girl is / are willing to join this competition.

2. Every Peter, Tom, and Harry has / have a mobile now-a-days.

3. **Every leaf and flower proclaim / proclaims the glory of God.**
