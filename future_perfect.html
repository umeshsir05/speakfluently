Future Perfect Tense

🌟 Future Perfect Tense (भविष्यत् पूर्ण काल)

🔹 Definition (परिभाषा)

It shows an action that will be completed before a certain time in the future.
यह उस क्रिया को दर्शाता है जो भविष्य में किसी निश्चित समय से पहले पूरी हो चुकी होगी।

👉 Keywords: by, by then, before, by tomorrow, by next year, etc.
👉 मुख्य शब्द: तब तक, उससे पहले, कल तक, अगले साल तक…

📝 Formula (संरचना)
1. Affirmative (सकारात्मक वाक्य)

👉 Subject + will have + V3 (Past Participle) + object

I will have finished my homework by 8 pm.
(मैं रात 8 बजे तक अपना होमवर्क पूरा कर चुका होऊँगा।)
2. Negative (नकारात्मक वाक्य)

👉 Subject + will not (won’t) have + V3 + object

She will not have reached the station by 9 am.
(वह सुबह 9 बजे तक स्टेशन नहीं पहुँची होगी।)
3. Interrogative (प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य)

👉 Will + subject + have + V3 + object ?

Will they have completed the project by tomorrow?
(क्या वे कल तक प्रोजेक्ट पूरा कर चुके होंगे?)
4. WH-Word + Interrogative (प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य with WH-word)

👉 WH-word + will + subject + have + V3 + object ?

What will you have learned by next month?
(आप अगले महीने तक क्या सीख चुके होंगे?)
Where will he have gone by then?
(वह तब तक कहाँ जा चुका होगा?)
Who will have cooked the food by 2 pm?
(दोपहर 2 बजे तक खाना किसने बना लिया होगा?)
🎯 Quick Recap (संक्षेप में)
Affirmative: Subject + will have + V3
Negative: Subject + will not have + V3
Interrogative: Will + subject + have + V3?
WH-Interrogative: WH + will + subject + have + V3?
