Verb (क्रियाएँ)

Verb is a doing word. जिस शब्द से किसी काम का होना या करना पाया जाता है, उसे Verb कहते हैं।

Types of Verbs

Modal Verbs: be, do, have, need, can, may, must, shall, dare, will, ought to, used to
Ordinary Verbs: to go, to write, to laugh, to sleep

Regular Verbs

Present (V1)Past (V2)Past Participle (V3)
WantWantedWanted
PlayPlayedPlayed
FinishFinishedFinished

Irregular Verbs

Present (V1)Past (V2)Past Participle (V3)
ComeCameCome
GoWentGone
EatAteEaten

Transitive Verb (सकर्मक क्रिया)

ऐसे Verbs जिन्हें अपना अर्थ स्पष्ट करने के लिए Object की आवश्यकता होती है।

Ex. – He plays cricket.
They broke the chairs.
She invited me to the party.

Intransitive Verb (अकर्मक क्रिया)

ऐसे Verbs जिन्हें अर्थ स्पष्ट करने के लिए Object की आवश्यकता नहीं होती।

Ex. – He is sleeping.
The sun is setting.
She died.
The girl ran down the street.
Birds fly flapping their wings.
