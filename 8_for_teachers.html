🎯 Questions Teachers Often Ask

Do you understand?
क्या तुम समझ गए?
Any questions?
कोई सवाल?
Can you repeat that?
क्या तुम उसे दोहरा सकते हो?
Who knows the answer?
जवाब कौन जानता है?
What do you think?
तुम क्या सोचते हो?
Why is this important?
यह क्यों महत्वपूर्ण है?
Can you give an example?
क्या तुम उदाहरण दे सकते हो?
Have you finished?
क्या तुमने पूरा कर लिया?
Teacher's Tip: Use a friendly but firm tone. Encourage students with positive phrases like "Good effort!" and "You can do it." Consistent use of these phrases will create an English-rich environment.

Use these phrases daily to make your classroom more interactive and English-friendly!
