Pronoun – Meaning and Types



What is a Pronoun?
A pronoun is a word used instead of a noun to avoid repeating the noun again and again.
Pronoun वह शब्द है जो noun के बदले में इस्तेमाल होता है।
Example
Ravi is my friend. He is very kind.
यहाँ He = Ravi (noun की जगह)
--- Types of Pronoun (सर्वनाम के प्रकार)
1. Personal Pronoun (व्यक्तिवाचक सर्वनाम)
Used for people or things.
लोगों या चीज़ों के लिए प्रयोग होता है।
Examples: I, we, you, he, she, it, they
She is a teacher.
They are playing.
2. Possessive Pronoun (अधिकार सूचक सर्वनाम)
Shows ownership.
किसी चीज़ पर अधिकार बताता है।
Examples: mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs
This book is mine.
That house is hers.
3. Demonstrative Pronoun (संकेतवाचक सर्वनाम)
Points to something.
किसी चीज़ की ओर इशारा करता है।
Examples: this, that, these, those
This is my pen.
Those are apples.
4. Interrogative Pronoun (प्रश्नवाचक सर्वनाम)
Used to ask questions.
प्रश्न पूछने के लिए।
Examples: who, what, which, whom
Who is your teacher?
What is your name?
5. Reflexive Pronoun (आत्मवाचक सर्वनाम)
Action comes back to the subject.
जब काम करने वाला खुद पर ही काम करे।
Examples: myself, himself, herself, ourselves
I did it myself.
He hurt himself.
--- Noun (संज्ञा) – Types with Examples
What is a Noun?
A noun is the name of a person, place, animal, or thing.
Noun किसी व्यक्ति, स्थान, वस्तु या जानवर का नाम होता है।
--- Types of Noun (संज्ञा के प्रकार)
1. Proper Noun (व्यक्तिवाचक संज्ञा)
Special name of a person or place.
किसी विशेष व्यक्ति या स्थान का नाम।
Examples: Ram, Delhi, India, Ganga
Ram lives in Delhi.
2. Common Noun (जातिवाचक संज्ञा)
General name.
सामान्य नाम।
Examples: boy, city, river, teacher
The boy is running.
3. Collective Noun (समूहवाचक संज्ञा)
Name of a group.
समूह का नाम।
Examples: team, class, crowd, army
Our class is very quiet.
4. Material Noun (द्रव्यवाचक संज्ञा)
Names of materials or substances.
पदार्थों के नाम।
Examples: gold, water, milk, iron
Gold is expensive.
5. Abstract Noun (भाववाचक संज्ञा)
Things we cannot see or touch.
जो महसूस की जाती हैं, देखी नहीं जातीं।
Examples: honesty, love, bravery, happiness
Honesty is the best policy.
--- Quick Tip for Kids
Noun = Name
Pronoun = Name’s replacement
