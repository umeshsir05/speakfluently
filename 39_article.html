Articles




1. All / Both / Double / Just + the + adjective + noun

All the famous singers
He will pay just the right amount.
Exactly the right color.
---
2. Such / What / Many / Quite / Rather + a/an + adjective + noun

Rather an interesting book
Quite an unlucky person
What a big fool!
Such an intelligent person
---
3. Article + Adverb + Adjective + Noun

A very intelligent boy / girl
A very beautiful girl
---
4. Position of Articles (Such, Many, What, Rather, Half)

Half a kilo
Half a dozen
Half an hour
What a girl / fool
---

5. All / Both / Double + the + noun

All the girls
Both the books
Double the amount
---

6. How / So / Too / As + adjective + a/an + noun

How beautiful a girl she is!

It is so fine a day.

---
7. a/an + collective noun + of + plural noun

A mob of angry people
A volley of stones / arrows
A bunch of crooks / keys
A congregation of worshippers
A syndicate of businessmen
