Articles



1. All / Both / Double / Just + the + adjective + noun Correct: All the famous singers He will pay just the right amount. Exactly the right color. --- 2. Such / What / Many / Quite / Rather + a/an + adjective + noun Correct: Rather an interesting book Quite an unlucky person What a big fool! Such an intelligent person --- 3. Article + Adverb + Adjective + Noun Correct: A very intelligent boy / girl A very beautiful girl --- 4. Position of Articles (Such, Many, What, Rather, Half) Correct: Half a kilo Half a dozen Half an hour What a girl / fool --- 5. All / Both / Double + the + noun Correct: All the girls Both the books Double the amount --- 6. How / So / Too / As + adjective + a/an + noun Correct: How beautiful a girl she is! It is so fine a day. (Your earlier version “How a beautiful girl she is!” was incorrect.) --- 7. a/an + collective noun + of + plural noun Correct: A mob of angry people A volley of stones / arrows A bunch of crooks / keys A congregation of worshippers A syndicate of businessmen ---
