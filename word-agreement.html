Subject-Verb Agreement (Syntax)

Agreement of the verb with its subject – Complete rules with examples

Basic Rule

Singular Subject के साथ Singular Verb तथा Plural Subject के साथ Plural Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ This mango is very sweet.
✗ This mango are very sweet.
✓ His dog barks at strangers.
✗ His dog bark at strangers.
✓ These apples are very sweet.
✗ These apples is very sweet.

Rule 2: Modal Verbs

Modal Verbs (will, shall, may, can, would, should, might, could, ought to, must, need not, dare not, used to) का प्रयोग Singular और Plural Subjects के साथ एक समान होता है।

✓ He can swim. / They can swim.
✓ She will come. / We will come.

Rule 3: Two or more Singular Subjects joined by 'and'

जब दो या दो से अधिक Singular Subjects ‘and’ से जुड़े हों, तो Plural Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Soni and Sheetu are going to the cinema.
✗ Soni and Sheetu is going to the cinema.

Rule 4: Two Singular Nouns joined by 'and' referring to the same person/thing

यदि दो Singular Nouns ‘and’ द्वारा जुड़े हों तथा वे एक ही व्यक्ति या वस्तु का बोध कराते हैं, तो Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है। (Article का प्रयोग केवल पहले Noun के साथ होता है।)

✓ The Principal and teacher has just gone.
✗ The Principal and teacher have just gone.
✓ A black and white cow is grazing.
✗ A black and white cow are grazing.
यदि दो भिन्न व्यक्तियों का अर्थ हो, तो Article प्रत्येक Noun के पहले आएगा और Verb Plural होगा।
✓ The Principal and the teacher have come.
✓ A black cow and a white cow are grazing.

Rule 5: Pairs of Nouns (Bread and butter, Slow and steady, etc.)

अंग्रेजी में कुछ Nouns ऐसे हैं जो एक जोड़ी में प्रयोग होते हैं – Bread and milk, Bread and butter, Slow and steady, Rice and curry, Horse and carriage, Pen and ink आदि। इनके साथ Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Slow and steady wins the race.
✗ Slow and steady win the race.
✓ Bread and milk is wholesome food.
✓ The horse and carriage is waiting for you.

Rule 6: 'Each' or 'Every' before compound subjects

यदि ‘and’ से जुड़े Singular Subjects के पहले ‘each’ या ‘every’ का प्रयोग हो, तो Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Every boy and girl was warned.
✗ Every boy and girl were warned.
✓ Each boy and each girl was rewarded.
✓ Every man, woman and child is happy.

Rule 7: 'or', 'nor', 'either...or', 'neither...nor'

Verb अपने निकटतम Subject (nearest) के अनुसार Singular या Plural होता है।

✓ Either he or I am at fault.
✓ Neither you nor he is at fault.
✓ Either students are able to do the sum.
✓ Either student is able to do the sum.
✓ Neither the parents nor the sons have come.
✓ Not only the director but also the students were present.

Rule 8: 'Each of / Either of / Neither of'

इनके बाद Plural Noun/Pronoun तथा Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Each of the boys has come.
✓ Either of the roads leads to the station.
✓ Neither of them is guilty.

Rule 9: Relative Pronouns (who, which, that)

Relative Clause का verb अपने Antecedent के अनुसार होता है।

✓ He is the boy who has helped me.
✗ He is the boy who have helped me.
✓ One of the best students that have come will be rewarded.
✓ You, who are my senior, should teach me.
✗ You, who is my senior, should teach me.

Rule 10: 'A number of' vs 'The number of'

A number of + Plural Noun + Plural Verb.
The number of + Plural Noun + Singular Verb.

✓ A number of boys have come.
✗ A number of boys has come.
✓ The number of students is increasing.
✗ The number of students are increasing.

Rule 11: 'Many', 'Many a', 'A good many'

Many, A good many, A great many → Plural Noun + Plural Verb.
Many a/an → Singular Noun + Singular Verb.

✓ Many students have participated.
✗ Many student has participated.
✓ Many a student has participated.
✓ A good many musicians have come.

Rule 12: Indefinite Pronouns (Everyone, Somebody, Nobody, Anything, etc.)

ये Singular होते हैं, इसलिए इनके साथ Singular Verb का प्रयोग होता है। परन्तु आधुनिक अंग्रेजी में Pronoun के लिए ‘them/their’ का प्रयोग स्वीकार्य है।

✓ Everyone was rewarded.
✗ Everyone were rewarded.
✓ Nobody knows the fact.
✓ Nothing was lost.

Rule 13: 'Few / A few / The few' and 'Little / A little / The little'

Few/A few/The few → Plural Countable Noun + Plural Verb.
Little/A little/The little → Uncountable Noun + Singular Verb.

✓ A few boys have come.
✗ A few boys has come.
✓ A little money was spent.
✗ A little money were spent.

Rule 14: Words like 'as well as, with, together with, along with, besides, etc.'

इनसे जुड़े Subjects के साथ Verb पहले Subject के अनुसार प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Sachin as well as his friends is coming.
✗ Sachin as well as his friends are coming.
✓ The teacher, with all his students, was present.
✓ Ashish, rather than you, has to go.
✓ She as well as her parents has come.

Rule 15: Collective Noun + of + Plural Noun

इस संरचना में Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ A band of musicians has come.
✗ A band of musicians have come.

Rule 16: Time, Distance, Amount

एक निश्चित समय, दूरी या रकम को बताने के लिए Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Fifty kilometers is a long distance.
✓ Ten thousand rupees is a big amount.
✗ Ten thousand rupees are a big amount.
परन्तु: Ten thousand rupees have been spent. (यहाँ धनराशि के रूप में नहीं, बल्कि individual notes के रूप में)

Rule 17: Nouns plural in form but singular in meaning (Mathematics, Physics, News, etc.)

इनके साथ Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Mathematics is a good subject.
✗ Mathematics are a good subject.
✓ The news was shocking.

Rule 18: Nouns singular in form but plural in meaning (Cattle, People, Police, Peasantry, etc.)

इनके साथ Plural Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ The cattle are grazing.
✗ The cattle is grazing.
✓ The police have arrived.

Rule 19: 'Others' vs 'Another'

Others → Plural Noun + Plural Verb.
Another → Singular Noun + Singular Verb.

✓ There are so many other hotels than the Maurya.
✓ There is another school near the Golghar.

Rule 20: 'The + Adjective' (The rich, The poor, The young, The old, The English, etc.)

पूरी जाति (class/group) का बोध कराते हैं, इसलिए इनके साथ Plural Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ The rich are not always happy.
✓ The poor need our help.

Rule 21: Quantifiers (Some, All, Most, Half, etc.)

इनके बाद आने वाले Noun के अनुसार Verb Singular या Plural होता है।

✓ Some water is needed.
✓ Some animals are faithful.
✓ All the money was stolen.
✓ All the students are laborious.

Rule 22: Phrases like 'Lots of', 'A lot of', 'Plenty of', 'Percent of', 'Half of', etc.

Verb उस Noun के अनुसार होता है जो इन Phrases के बाद आता है।

✓ A lot of books were published.
✓ A lot of milk was bought.
✓ Plenty of sugar is required.

Rule 23: Nouns that are always plural (Trousers, Scissors, Spectacles, Shoes, etc.)

इनके साथ Plural Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है। परन्तु ‘A pair of’ से पहले Singular Verb आता है।

✓ My shoes are new.
✗ My shoe is new.
✓ The scissors are sharp.
✓ A pair of scissors was bought.
✗ A pair of scissors were bought.

Rule 24: 'More than one'

More than one + Singular Noun + Singular Verb.
More ___ than one में Plural Verb आता है।

✓ More than one student was absent.
✗ More than one students were absent.
✓ More books than one have been bought.

Rule 25: Repetition of Nouns with Preposition (Man after man, Month after month)

ऐसे वाक्यों में Singular Verb का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

✓ Man after man is coming here.
✓ Month after month has passed.

Rule 26: 'There' and 'It' as Introductory Subjects

‘There’ के बाद Verb उस Noun के अनुसार होता है जो ‘there’ के बाद आता है।
‘It’ हमेशा Singular Verb लेता है।

✓ There was an old farmer.
✓ There are five farmers.
✗ There is five farmers.
✓ It is these students that came on time.

Rule 27: Subjunctive Mood (I wish, If, As if, As though)

वर्तमान की अवास्तविक इच्छा या स्थिति के लिए ‘were’ का प्रयोग किया जाता है – चाहे Subject Singular हो या Plural।

✓ She wishes she were a bird.
✗ She wishes she was a bird.
✓ If I were you, I would go.
✓ If she were you, she would do it.

Rule 28: Inversion with Adverbs (Hardly, Seldom, Rarely, Never, etc.)

इनसे शुरू होने वाले वाक्यों में Verb Subject से पहले आता है, और वह Subject के अनुसार Singular या Plural होता है।

✓ Hardly does she go to college.
✓ Seldom do they come to me.
✓ Under a tree were lying tired workers.
Summary: Subject-Verb Agreement के सभी नियमों को ध्यान से पढ़ें और उदाहरणों के साथ अभ्यास करें। प्रतियोगी परीक्षाओं में यह topic बहुत महत्वपूर्ण है।
