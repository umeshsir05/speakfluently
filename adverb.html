Adverb (क्रिया-विशेषण)

Adverb एक ऐसा शब्द है जो किसी Verb, Adjective या दूसरे Adverb की विशेषता बताता है।

Examples:
1. He runs fast. (fast – adverb of manner)
2. He runs very fast. (very – adverb modifying another adverb)

Special Adverbs (Noun/Pronoun की विशेषता)

कुछ Adverb Noun या Pronoun की विशेषता भी बताते हैं: Even, Only, Almost, At least

Examples:
1. Only he can solve this problem. (केवल वही)
2. Even he can solve this problem. (वह भी)
3. No one can change the history, not even God.
4. At least you should participate in the competition.

Types of Adverbs

1. Adverb of Manner (रीति-वाचक)

कार्य करने की विधि या तरीका दर्शाता है। प्रायः Adjective में ly जोड़ने से बनता है।

Examples: Anger → Angrily, Bad → Badly, Easy → Easily
He writes beautifully.

2. Adverb of Time (समय-वाचक)

समय का बोध कराता है।

Examples: Now, then, soon, before, daily, yesterday, today, tomorrow, last month, next week, recently, shortly.
He came yesterday. / Today he will come.

3. Adverb of Place (स्थान-वाचक)

स्थान का बोध कराता है।

Examples: Here, there, in, out, away, indoor, outdoor, near, far, above, below, everywhere.
There was a king. / He went there.

4. Adverb of Frequency (आवृत्ति-वाचक)

कार्य करने की आवृत्ति (कितनी बार) दर्शाता है।

Examples: Once, twice, always, never, often, seldom, again, rarely, many times, already.
I always go there. / He has already gone.

Position of Adverbs

  • Adverb को उस शब्द के जितना संभव हो उतना पास रखना चाहिए जिसकी वह विशेषता बता रहा हो।
  • Adverb of manner, time, place का प्रयोग सामान्यतः Verb के बाद होता है। यदि Object हो तो Object के बाद।
    He writes beautifully. / He writes a letter beautifully.
  • Adverb of time और place को वाक्य के शुरू में भी रख सकते हैं।
    Today he will come. / There was a king.
  • Adverb of frequency का प्रयोग मुख्य Verb से पहले होता है, लेकिन never, already, always, often, once, seldom, twice का प्रयोग main verb के बाद भी हो सकता है।
    I have never seen the Taj. / He has already gone. / I take tea twice a day.

Formation of Adverbs

  • सामान्यतः Adjective में ly लगाकर Adverb बनाया जाता है।
    Slow → Slowly, Quick → Quickly
  • कभी भी दो Adjectives एक साथ प्रयोग नहीं होते – पहले Adjective को Adverb बनाना चाहिए।
    It is real important (✗) → It is really important (✓)
    It is absolute right (✗) → It is absolutely right (✓)
  • निम्न शब्दों के अंत में ly नहीं लगाया जाता क्योंकि ये Adjective और Adverb दोनों हैं:
    Hard, Fast, Late, So, Straight, Well
    He is walking fastly (✗) → He is walking fast (✓)
    Hard (कठोर) vs Hardly (मुश्किल से)
  • निम्न शब्दों के बाद Adverb की जगह Adjective का प्रयोग होता है:
    Smell, felt, taste, seem, appear
    She seems angrily (✗) → She seems angry (✓)
    The roses smell sweetly (✗) → The roses smell sweet (✓)

Uses of Some Important Adverbs

Hardly / Scarcely ...... when / No Sooner ...... than

इनका प्रयोग Conjunction के रूप में भी होता है।

Hardly had I reached the station when the train left.
No sooner did he see me than he ran away.

Rather

To के साथ rather का प्रयोग किया जाता है।

He would rather die than beg.

Enough (पर्याप्त)

Enough का प्रयोग Adverb के रूप में होता है, तब इसकी स्थिति Adjective + Enough होती है।

She is beautiful enough to attract anyone.
Note: Adverbs add depth to your sentences. Remember the position rules – manner, place, time usually after the verb; frequency before the main verb (except with 'to be' and auxiliaries). Avoid double adjectives – convert the first to adverb.
