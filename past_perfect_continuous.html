🎯 Past Perfect Continuous Tense क्या है?

🌟 Past Perfect Continuous Tense (भूतकाल पूर्ण अपूर्ण काल)

🔹 Structure (संरचना)

✅ Subject + had been + Verb(-ing) + Object + since/for + time

1. Positive Sentences (सकारात्मक वाक्य)

👉 Structure: Subject + had been + Verb(-ing) + Object + since/for + time

I had been studying for two hours. मैं दो घंटे से पढ़ रहा था।
She had been cooking since morning. वह सुबह से खाना बना रही थी।
They had been playing cricket for three hours. वे तीन घंटे से क्रिकेट खेल रहे थे।
2. Negative Sentences (नकारात्मक वाक्य)

👉 Structure: Subject + had not been + Verb(-ing) + Object + since/for + time

I had not been studying for two hours. मैं दो घंटे से नहीं पढ़ रहा था।
She had not been cooking since morning. वह सुबह से खाना नहीं बना रही थी।
They had not been playing cricket for three hours. वे तीन घंटे से क्रिकेट नहीं खेल रहे थे।
3. Interrogative Sentences (प्रश्नवाचक वाक्य)

(a) Yes/No Type Questions (हाँ/ना वाले प्रश्न)
👉 Structure: Had + Subject + been + Verb(-ing) + Object + since/for + time?

Had I been studying for two hours? क्या मैं दो घंटे से पढ़ रहा था?
Had she been cooking since morning? क्या वह सुबह से खाना बना रही थी?
Had they been playing cricket for three hours? क्या वे तीन घंटे से क्रिकेट खेल रहे थे?

(b) Wh-Questions (प्रश्न शब्दों से शुरू वाले)
👉 Structure: Wh-word + had + Subject + been + Verb(-ing) + Object + since/for + time?

What had you been doing since morning? तुम सुबह से क्या कर रहे थे?
Where had she been working for five years? वह पाँच साल से कहाँ काम कर रही थी?
Why had they been shouting for an hour? वे एक घंटे से क्यों चिल्ला रहे थे?
✅ Summary (सारांश)
Positive → had been + V(-ing)
Negative → had not been + V(-ing)
Interrogative → Had + Subject + been + V(-ing)?
